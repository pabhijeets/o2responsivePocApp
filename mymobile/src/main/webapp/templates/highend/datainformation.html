What data is included in my data allowance?

All of the data that goes to and from your mobile over our network, except when you use Wi-Fi, is included in your allowance.

This includes web pages, videos, downloading applications and sending and receiving emails. Your data usage is updated every 30 minutes on the My O2 app.

Is there anything that isn't included in my data allowance?

Yes. We don't include some of our main services, like:

- Visual Voicemail and Palm Pre software updates

- Non O2 UK data (when you're roaming abroad) which is charged separately

- Access to key O2 websites including My O2

So you can keep using them even if you run out of data.

What happens when I've used up all my allowance?

We'll send you a text if you use up all your data. Your data speeds will slow down to 15kbps.

So you'll still be able to look at mobile web pages, but they'll take longer to load.

If you're on Web Daily, and you reach your 100MB daily allowance, you'll have to wait until midnight before you can use any more data.
